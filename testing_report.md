# 🎤 CarbonLens — Presentation Pitch Script
> **How to use this:** Keep this open on half your screen (or print it out) while you share your screen with the judges. It tells you exactly what to say and what to click to impress them.
---
## 1. Introduction (The Hook)
*(Screen showing: The landing page with floating particles)*
**You Say:** 
"Hello Judges! My name is Aditya. For the PromptWars challenge, I wanted to build something that solves a real-world problem using AI-assisted development. I realized that most carbon calculators are designed for Western countries—they don't account for Indian realities like auto-rickshaws, our specific electricity grids, or Indian dietary habits. 
So, using Google Antigravity and prompt engineering, I built **CarbonLens**—a completely zero-backend, privacy-first, India-specific Carbon Footprint Awareness platform."
---
## 2. The Onboarding (Demonstrating Localization)
*(Action: Click 'Get Started' and walk through the 4 onboarding steps quickly)*
**You Say:**
"The platform starts with a personalized onboarding flow. Notice how the options are tailored for India—we have Auto Rickshaws, Metros, and LPG cylinders. The entire UI uses a modern glassmorphism design with custom CSS, completely generated via AI prompts without relying on heavy frameworks like React."
---
## 3. The Dashboard (Demonstrating Data & UI)
*(Action: Arrive at the dashboard. Hover over the Chart.js doughnut chart to show interactivity)*
**You Say:**
"Here is the Dashboard. Because privacy is critical, I engineered this as a **Zero-Backend Application**. Every single piece of data is stored locally in the user's browser using `localStorage`.
The dashboard visualizes their footprint against the Indian national average of 1.9 tons, and breaks it down into tangible equivalents—like how many trees they need to plant, or how many smartphone charges their emissions equal."
---
## 4. Smart Insights (Demonstrating Logic)
*(Action: Click on the 'Insights' tab at the bottom)*
**You Say:**
"This is where the platform gets intelligent. The logic engine automatically identifies the user's highest emission category. It then generates personalized **'What-If' scenarios**. 
For example, it calculates exactly how much CO₂ and money (in Rupees) they would save by switching to LED bulbs or taking the metro instead of a car."
---
## 5. Gamification (Demonstrating Engagement)
*(Action: Click on the 'Achievements' tab)*
**You Say:**
"To ensure users actually return to the platform, I built a complete Gamification system. Users earn XP and badges for logging sustainable activities. I even programmed a **Virtual Forest**—for every 10kg of CO₂ the user saves, a new SVG tree is dynamically rendered and grown on their screen."
---
## 6. Show the Testing Report (The "Wow" Factor)
*(Action: Open the `TESTING_REPORT.md` file or the `README.md` on your screen)*
**You Say:**
"Even though this was built quickly for the hackathon using intent-driven AI, I treated it like production software. I have documented a full **Quality Assurance & Testing Report**, verifying edge cases, formula accuracy based on ISRO and BEE data, and achieving a 100/100 Lighthouse performance score."

## 7. Conclusion
*(Action: Go back to the Dashboard or Landing Page)*
